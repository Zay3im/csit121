Sherdog.com Evaluation

Website info and target audience

Sherdog is an MMA news and information website. The URL of this website is https://www.sherdog.com/. The name of the website is Sherdog. Its target audience are MMA fans, people looking for fighter statistics, combat sport followers, and people who watch organizations like UFC, Bellator, PFL, and Ultimate Fighting Championship. Sherdog uses hierarchical organization with main categories and top navigation menu.

Audit score and site effectiveness

  • Sherdog got an audit score of 10% according to the Accessibility Checker. The audit found 186 critical issues, 40 passed audits, and 22 items that need manual review.
  • I personally think the site is fairly effective. It is good for quick access to lots of MMA information in one place. The navigation menu is also easy to navigate and locate different sections.
  • There can be a lot to take in when first getting into the website. It can feel a bit crowded. Other than that you are still able to complete tasks and find fight results and many more.

Engagement

Sherdog is engaging for MMA fans along with myself being one. It has everything an MMA fan would love to see. It has fight coverage, news, interviews, rankings, and many more. The website has a dark color scheme and white bold headlines that have nice contrast. The site gives off an intense gritty competitive feel as the combat sport does. The site has exciting pictures and news of upcoming fights and nice in action pictures of fighters that pop on the pages. It has very up-to-date news when it comes to upcoming events and fighters encouraging users to browse. The only bad thing is there is a good amount of advertisements and cluttered sections that can lower the user experience.

Recommendations

One recommendation to improve would be to focus on the accessibility. The audit showed many accessibility problems like low contrast, missing alt text on images, inaccessible buttons, and keyboard navigation issues. Improving these will make the site easier for people with disabilities and to be compliant under United States law. One more improvement I would make would be reducing clutter on the homepage. It has a lot of content mixed in with ads on one page which can be overwhelming for the user. I think adding more spacing and making some sections smaller will improve and create a cleaner user experience.
