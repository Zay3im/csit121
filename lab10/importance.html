Why is it important?

WebAuthn is important because passwords are not always safe. People forget them, make them too simple, or reuse the same password more than one place. If one account gets hacked, other accounts can also be at risk.

This also helps stop phishing attacks. Phishing is when a fake website tricks someone into typing their login information. WebAuthn helps stop this because the login is connected to the real website.

I think this matters because everybody has online accounts now. School, banking, social media, and email all need strong security. WebAuthn helps make logging in safer without making it too difficult for the user.
